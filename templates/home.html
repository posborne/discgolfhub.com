Welcome

Welcome. Disc Golf Hub is the place for disc golfers across the world to find, rate, and comment on over 3000 courses, keep track of scores, and communicate with other lovers of the game.

The site uses Facebook connect to provide seemless integration with your facebook account and your disc golfing life. No separate registration is needed, you can just use your existing facebook account.

What do you think?

So, what do you think? Development is active and your input is more than welcome! In order to comment you must login (using Facebook connect) at the top of the page. Be sure to uncheck posting to your facebook profile unless you want to share your love!
